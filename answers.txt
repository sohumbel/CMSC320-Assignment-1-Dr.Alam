Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be applied to a wide range of fields, including healthcare. For example, in medical research, data science can be used to analyze vast amounts of patient data to detect patterns, predict disease outbreaks, or personalize treatments.
